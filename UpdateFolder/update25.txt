Title: USA’s ICE is getting things done!
Date: 23 Dec 2026

In an unprecedented and highly controversial move, the United States, under the expanded authority of its Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), has reportedly carried out a mass expulsion of undocumented and temporary-status immigrants, effectively removing millions from the country within a short span of time.

The policy, widely criticised by international observers, has triggered what analysts are calling a global migration shock, as displaced populations begin seeking entry into other nations.

With limited options available, many are now expected to move toward Europe. Early reports suggest that the United Kingdom may become a primary destination, with crossings across the English Channel anticipated to rise sharply in the coming weeks.

One migration expert remarked, “You don’t just remove millions of people and expect them to disappear, they will go somewhere.”

Authorities are already preparing for a potential surge in arrivals, raising urgent questions about border control, asylum policy, and the country’s capacity to respond.
