Title: BREAKING NEWS 
Date: 18 Mar 2027

London, UK — A major fire has broken out at the House of Commons chamber, part of the historic Palace of Westminster, prompting a large-scale emergency response and raising fresh concerns about political tensions in the country.
According to initial reports from emergency services, the blaze began after a horde of protestors set fire to the chambers. This follows after protests had been taking place in central London earlier in the day, with demonstrators criticising what they described as “stupid, useless policies” that “do nothing but waste tax payer's money”. 
In a devastating and tragic development, there is only 1 known survivor; all other Members of Parliament present in the chamber at the time of the fire are reported to have died, marking an unimaginable loss of political leadership and triggering a constitutional crisis unlike any before. Nick Timothy was reportedly at an unspecified island when the fire broke out, making him the only known surviving MP from the incident.
