Title: The Shitp has sunk
Date: 31 July 2026

Following the vomiting incident between Prime Minister Keir Starmer and President Trump, President Trump has increased tariffs rate on United Kingdom to 1000%. 

To make up to President Trump, the Prime Minister bought a McDonalds’ Big Mac meal from the largest Mcdonald’s outlet in the United States for his lunch. Following his lunch, the Prime Minister headed to the White House to apologise to President Trump.
However, during the visit, Prime Minister Starmer experienced a further gastrointestinal mishap, resulting in a blockage in the White House facilities, an event reportedly described by aides as “an awkward diplomatic moment.”

President Trump, upon witnessing the incident, reportedly shook his head and remained unimpressed, suggesting that the United Kingdom’s efforts at reconciliation have so far failed to make an impact. Observers note that the episode has added a new layer of challenge to an already strained diplomatic relationship.

'I think it might have something to do with the Big Mac meal that I ate, but I cannot be certain. Regardless, I apologise to all Britons for my mishap and it might be time to consider other trading options.'

Surprisingly, public opinion in the UK has not turned strongly against Prime Minister Keir Starmer, as many Britons are more focused on President Donald Trump’s recent troubles in the ongoing US-Iran conflict.
