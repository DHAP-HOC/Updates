Title: Taxes, Yay or Nay
Date: 20 July 2026

With many delegates within the House of Commons proposing an increase in taxes for the rich and ultra-rich in order to improve the welfare of all citizens, debate has intensified across the political spectrum. The current Prime Minister even argued that the previous Prime Minister had implemented similar measures, and that this precedent suggests the current government could do the same.

Business leaders have warned that higher corporate taxation and wealth taxes could make the UK less attractive for investment compared with other global financial and technology hubs. Some executives have privately expressed concerns about the long-term stability of the British business environment, particularly following months of political turbulence and economic uncertainty.

Rumours has it that the following large-scale companies are planning to relocate out of United Kingdom: ScottishPower Renewables, Microsoft, Rolls-Royce Holdings and Glaxosmithkline

More updates to be followed.
