Title: Nick Timothy? More like Nick Timonkids
Date: 3 Feb 2027

On 2 Feb 2027, Sir Nick Timothy was found on a very romantic date with a young lady who was identified to be Robert Jenrick’s daughter. 

According to onlookers, the pair were seen sharing a meal of Wingstop’s notably spicy Atomic Wings, with reports of affectionate behaviour, including hand-holding under the table. The sighting has since circulated widely online, prompting debate over professionalism and personal conduct among political figures.

While no official statements have been released by either party, the incident has raised questions among the public regarding optics and judgement, particularly given the current political climate.
