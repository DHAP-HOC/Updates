Title: BREAKING UPDATE – Major Pharmaceutical Firm Relocates Headquarters
Date: 2 Apr 2026

A major pharmaceutical company, AstraZeneca, has announced it will relocate its global headquarters to Sweden, citing concerns about long-term economic stability and investment conditions in the United Kingdom.

Approximately 9,000 UK jobs will be cut, leaving only a small research and manufacturing facility operating in Cambridge. Economists warn that this move could trigger further layoffs across the biotechnology and pharmaceutical sectors.

The government has stated that it will work with unions to ensure adequate severance pay for affected workers.

More updates are to be followed.

This article was written by The Guardian.
