Title: The Great Malaysian Earthquake
Date: 16 July 2026

On 15 July 2026, at 08:20 local time, a magnitude 8.4 earthquake struck Malaysia, causing massive destruction over hundreds of kilometres.

Initial reports suggest that a large portion of Malaysia’s semiconductor assembly capacity has been shut down indefinitely, disrupting global technology supply chains. Technology manufacturers worldwide are now scrambling to find alternative chip production sites.

This article was written by BBC
