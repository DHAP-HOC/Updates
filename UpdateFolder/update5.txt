Title: Survey - “Work is not worthwhile?!”
Date: 5 May 2026

More than two-thirds of Britons do not believe that work is worthwhile! According to a survey commissioned by our newspaper with a sample size of 100, most Britons believe that their time can be better spent than doing menial labour.

Said by one responder: “Cleaning toilets is so simple that even my dog could do it!” 

Meanwhile, voter confidence in Reform UK and The Conservative Party has generally rebounded.

This article was written by The Daily Mail.
