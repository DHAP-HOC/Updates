Title: BREAKING – TRELLICK TOWER ON FIRE
Date: 8 Jan 2027

London — A major fire has broken out at the Trellick Tower, one of the city’s most iconic residential buildings, prompting a large-scale emergency response overnight.

Fire services were dispatched within minutes, but the blaze spread rapidly through multiple floors. Authorities have confirmed 113 dead, 330 casualties and 5 missing, with emergency teams continuing search and rescue operations as the situation develops.

Residents from surrounding areas have been evacuated as a precaution, while hospitals across London have been placed on high alert to receive the injured.

Officials have yet to determine the cause of the fire, though early reports suggest potential structural and safety concerns may have contributed to the rapid spread.

One elderly passerby said: “It must be those damn irresponsible immigrants who caused the fire!”

The government has urged the public to avoid speculation and allow emergency services to carry out their work.

More updates are to follow.
