Title: Curiosity killed the cat
Date: 24 Jan 2027

In loving memory of Larry TheCat, the racist, hyper conservative man.

As of 23 Jan 2027, Larry TheCat has unfortunately passed.

Donald Trump has made a tribute on his death: “Larry was a great man, a very great man, people are saying it, many people, very smart people. He had tremendous instincts, really tremendous, like nobody’s ever seen before. Strong, very strong, believed in what he believed, maybe more than anyone, honestly.”

The cause of death was reportedly a medical disease. Larry TheCat had been suffering from an STD that he contracted after visiting an unspecified island.

A statement from his uncle: “Despite Larry’s controversial views, he was deeply committed to upholding the standards of Parliament. He often said, ‘It is alright to have fun, but in a parliamentary setting, one must ensure that everyone remains serious and genuinely cares about the welfare of the country.’”
