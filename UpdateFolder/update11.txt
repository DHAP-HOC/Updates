Title: Third Reshuffle of Ministers
Date: 20 Jun 2026

After yet another attempted VONC of the Prime Minister, there were some movements of positions within the council.

Shabana Mahmood has been expelled from the Labour Party after a vote of no confidence towards the Prime Minister against the 3 line whip.

Replacing his position as Home Secretary is Jonathan Reynolds.

Quoting the Prime Minister : “Jonathan Reynolds has proven to be a loyal party member. He is also capable and proven as a politician and a man of the people.”

Rumours have it that Shabana Mahmood has joined the Independent Alliance party.
