Title: PRITI IS OUT! 
Date: 20 Sep 2026

Kemi Badenoch has announced in a recent internal party meeting that Dame Priti Patel is expelled from the Conservative party with no further elaboration. Could this signal a fracturing in internal party relations for the Conservatives? There is now only two independent delegates in the HOC: Diane Abbott, and now, Dame Priti Patel.

In other news, the London Metropolitan Police has reported that the Leader of the Opposition’s house was vandalised by a short Indian woman last night. Police investigations are ongoing. Many speculate that this may be an act of defiance and outrage with the Leader of Opposition failing to do anything substantial in the HOC to further the Conservative's position. 

Meanwhile, with Prime Minister Keir Starmer pushing forward the Labour Bill, the Labour Party appears to be taking a more active role in driving the council, using a threatening statement that he will table all other bills if his labour bill does not go through. However, many in parliament believe Labour might be too late..
