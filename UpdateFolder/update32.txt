Title: New Contender…?
Date: 23 Jan 2027

Larry TheCat, a prominent former trade union leader and business person has formed a new party called Catfederation also known as Larry. TheCat had previously stood as an independent candidate for Clacton was largely seen as the precursor to the populist appeal of Nigel Farage. After losing the election to Giles Watling he had withdrawn from public life completely until now in a new press conference.

TheCat has called for the dissolution of the House of Commons and to replace the entire British democracy with a direct monarchy with him in charge. He has gotten popular support within the British Populace as Britons increasingly view democracy as unviable due to the constant rotation of Prime Ministers.

TheCat has called for extremely racist policies such as DNA testing for every British citizen and an immediate deportation of those that are not purely Anglo-Saxon. This has resulted in widespread panic within those who have immigrant backgrounds.

TheCat is largely seen as extreme by Political Scientists. Experts warn that should the Commons not find a solution to the broken immigration system especially a change in parties and votes of no confidence are passed, TheCat may try to gain power by force via redoing the March on Rome to London which may result in the King pushing all power to TheCat.

Sir Keir Starmer, the former Prime Minister has been contacted for a statement that he has made in support of TheCat, however we have not received any response from his office or the Jerry Party.
