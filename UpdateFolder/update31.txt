Title: Merz's Germany leaves the ECHR. Reports coming from Germany of Jewish individuals being rounded up.
Date: 20 January 2027

<i> Report from the BBC </i>

The German Government has announced their decision to leave the ECHR. In a bizarre press conference, German Chancellor Friedrich Merz was seen spotting a rectangular mustache, drawing parallels to his predecessor from 9 decades ago. 

With their resignation from the ECHR, ECHR officers have been expelled from the country. In addition, reports from journalists in the BBC has reported that men with ‘Jewish-looking’ features have been rounded up in trucks and sent to 'education facilities’ in the far south.

With this, far-right politicians have had renewed confidence in the ECHR as a body to protect human rights in Europe. Particularly, U.S. President Donald Trump has called upon all European states to remain the ECHR.
