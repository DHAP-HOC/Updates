Title: The rise of Jeremy Corbyn? Green Party leader defects to Your Party!
Date: 22 May 2026

After insider talks with Jeremy Corbyn, the rumours have been proven true, and leader of the Greens, Zack Polanski has defected to Your Party. This has come at the heels of a major protest at Westminster which saw the British People revolting against the British ‘uniparty’. Although the protestors were supportive of Corbyn's leadership, as he was able to coalesce support for his solutions, some are wondering whether he has taken it too far, by influencing the leader of the rising left-wing party to abandon his party machine. 

Former leader of the Green Party, Carla Denyer, has assumed control of the party. But as her fellow partymen have been absent from Westminster for the past week, some are wondering, is this the end of the Green Party?
