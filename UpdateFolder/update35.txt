Title: PM Carla Denyer sparks public outrage in recent scandal 
Date: 1 Feb 2027
Claims surfaced from unverified sources that Carla Denyer had taken a 40-second flight in her private jet from Singapore Seletar Airport to Singapore Changi Airport during her vacation to Singapore. Once the leader of the Green Party, many are angered by her hypocrisy - championing for green solutions whilst she herself spends extravagantly on herself. 

When interviewed, a member of public proclaimed, “WHAT A BIG FAT LIAR I DON'T KNOW WHY I VOTED FOR HIM”
