Title: Internal Party Split
Date: 3 Jan 2027

Westminster — In a dramatic turn of events within the House of Commons, a major political realignment has taken place. As of 2 January 2027, 23:59, the Left Coalition has officially fractured, with a group of prominent members breaking away to form a new political party.

The members leaving the Left Coalition are:
Jeremy Corbyn (L)
Shockat Adam (W)
Dan Carden
David Lammy
Diane Abbott
Ed Miliband
Lisa Nandy

And they have formed a new party called Our Party.

The official new Prime Minister is now Nigel Farage from the Bryan Party.
