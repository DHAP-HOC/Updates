Title: The rise of a worrisome protest
Date: 2 Sep 2026

Somerset — A coordinated effort by the the supporters of the Green Party has resulted in widespread protests across major cities. More specifically, they have formed a massive blockade at the entrance of the construction site of The Hinkley Point C
“Say NO TO NUCLEAR!” has emerged as the central message of the movement, with protestors calling for a shift towards renewable energy alternatives instead of nuclear expansion.
The nationwide protests have remained relatively peaceful (despite the blockade). The atmosphere is hopeful, with a dash of pessimism. Protestors fear that the agenda of revitalisation, aggressively pushed for by the Conservatives during election campaigning, could come at the expense of long-term environmental sustainability.
“Are we making the rich richer so that the poor can suffer to the irresponsible manner of using energy.” said one protestor.
